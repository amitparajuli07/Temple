Lord Tirupati Balaji

Lord Tirupati Balaji, also known as Venkateswara, is a revered form of Lord Vishnu. He is the presiding deity of the Venkateswara Temple in Tirumala, Andhra Pradesh.

This temple is one of the world's most visited pilgrimage sites. Believers say he incarnated to save humanity in the Kali Yuga. Devotees seek his blessings for wealth and spiritual well-being.

His iconography includes the tilaka on the forehead and sacred symbols like the Chakra and Shankh. His shrine is considered a divine source of energy and devotion.
